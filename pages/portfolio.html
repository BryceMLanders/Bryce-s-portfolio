Video Game Sales

This project was a fun project Bryce created using a D3 database with a Flask Backend. The data was retrieved online and stored in MySQL database. Once the information was scrubbed and cleanned up, I created various charts to reflect Sales by year, Sales by Publisher, Sales by Platform and the overall sales throughout video games lifetime. The charts are all interactive and entertaining compare your favorite Video Games, Genre, Publisher, or Platform. Once on the site, locate the 'Start Tour' button for more information.
